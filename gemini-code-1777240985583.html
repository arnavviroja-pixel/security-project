Learn how to protect yourself from scams

What is Identity Theft?

Identity theft happens when someone steals your personal information like your name, passwords, or credit card details and uses it without permission.

Common Types:

  • Phishing emails
  • Fake websites
  • Data breaches
  • Social engineering

Real-Life Examples:

  • Someone opens a credit card in your name
  • Hackers access your social media account
  • A scammer pretends to be your bank

Warning Signs:

  • Unfamiliar charges on your account
  • Passwords suddenly stop working
  • You receive alerts for accounts you didn’t create

How to Stay Safe:

  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Avoid suspicious links
  • Check for HTTPS
  • Never share personal information online
